System Modules

Our comprehensive church management system includes specialized modules to address all aspects of parish administration.

Events Management

A comprehensive calendar system to manage all parish events, masses, sacraments, and activities with scheduling, reminders, and attendance tracking.

Project Management

Track parish projects, renovations, and initiatives with budget management, progress updates, and contribution tracking features.

Parishioner Database

Maintain comprehensive records of families and individuals, including sacramental history, contact information, and ministry participation.

Ministry Coordination

Organize and schedule various church ministries, manage volunteer rosters, and coordinate roles for liturgical events.

Communication Tools

Send announcements, updates, and newsletters to specific groups or the entire parish through multiple channels.

Resource Management

Manage parish resources, facility scheduling, equipment checkout, and maintenance records in one central location.
